Panels should be though of as generic block containers that are likely to display content distinct from it's surrounds (e.g. has a border and/or background).

Note what appears to be large borders around the first two panels are actually a container background to illustrate panels are often white.

As usual, backgrounds and borders should be applied with the decoration classes.
