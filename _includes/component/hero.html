Use of headings and slide titles

Since Heros annd Carousels generally appear above the main page title (h1) it's not appropriate for a slide title to be a h element. However, in at least one case (main homepage) there's no other appropriat place for a main page titel to sit, so it's ok for the slide title to be the page title (h1). This is an exception rather than the rule. The downside here is that the slides won't have headings so can't be navigated in that way, but I think it's the best compromise.
